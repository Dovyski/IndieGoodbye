Come again? What this is all about

You probably have been working on a pet project, that amazing game you designed and coded with all the inner passion you could put together. It's missing just a few tweaks/levels/features/sfx/etc that are waiting for you to find some free time to finish them all.

Guess what? The end of the world has come, it's now or NEVER!

Help, please! What do you need to finish the game?

Art

Check this list for free assets. You also can try OpenGameArt.org (lots of free assets)..

Sound/Music

Look at FreeSound.org or FlashKit SoundFX. They have free SFX and musics. Fiverr is a nice place too.

Monetization

Pay the bills with: iAd and In-App Purchase for iOS, AdMob and In-App Billing for Android.

Free code

Looking for physics, animation or game engines? Search by category or by license, such as MIT.

Mobile integration

Check this amazing native extensions: Game Center and GoViral (Facebook, Twitter, Email).

Ranking, score and tests

Try Scoreoid for score/ranking, it's free and easy to use. For testing, use TestFlight.
